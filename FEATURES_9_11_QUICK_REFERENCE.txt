FEATURE #11: Dark/Light Mode Toggle — User-Friendly Theme Switch

QUICK USER GUIDE
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

IMPORTING DATA:
   1. Save old data as CSV (from previous export or spreadsheet)
   2. Open Settings → Backup & Restore
   3. Tap "Import from CSV"
   4. Select the CSV file
   5. Choose data type:
      - "Production Data" for daily egg logs
      - "Flock Inventory" for chicken records
   6. Wait for import to complete
   7. Check results (imported count, skipped, errors)
   8. See data instantly in app

SWITCHING THEMES:
   1. Open Settings → Backup & Restore
   2. Look at top card "Appearance"
   3. See current mode: 🌙 Dark Mode or ☀️ Light Mode
   4. Tap the toggle switch
   5. Watch app theme change instantly
   6. Preference saved automatically
   7. App remembers your choice next time
